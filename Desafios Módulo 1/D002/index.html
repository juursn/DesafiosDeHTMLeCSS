Rules of fight Club

  • 4th rule: Only two guys to a fight.

About the movie

Fight Club is a 1999 American film directed by David Fincher, based on the 1996 novel of the same name by Chuck Palahniuk. The film stars Edward Norton as an unnamed protagonist who forms an underground fight club with soap salesman Tyler Durden (Brad Pitt). The film explores themes of consumerism, identity, and self-destruction.
